### Interpretation Guide

- **Navigation Sequences & Answer Patterns**: Cheating groups (especially high/medium severity) should exhibit very similar `Nav Sequence` and `Answer Pattern (C/X)` strings among members. Honest users will be more varied.
- **Revisits**: Similar revisit patterns (specific questions revisited similar number of times) can be indicative if coupled with other similarities.
- **Timing Patterns**:
  - `Start Time`: Cheating group members might show staggered starts based on `Timing Start Delay` config.
  - `Duration (s)` & `Avg Interaction Time (s)`: Reflects `Completion Speed`. Lower `StdDev Interaction Time (s)` for cheaters indicates consistent pacing.
  - `Interactions Detail`: Look for similar durations on the same sequence of questions among group members.
- **Transition Time Correlation**: Values close to 1.0 for user pairs within a cheating group suggest highly synchronized progression through the quiz, a strong indicator of real-time collusion.
- **Grade**: While not a sole indicator, consistently high grades among a group with other similarities can be corroborative.
